Getting Started

How do I download BQ?

BQ is available for Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, and Android. Visit the download page to get started.

How do I set up BQ?

After downloading, follow the setup wizard. BQ will guide you through the configuration process.

Using BQ

How do I use BQ during an interview?

Simply press Ctrl+Enter (or Cmd+Enter on Mac) to get instant AI assistance. BQ will provide concise, natural answers.

Is BQ detectable?

No. BQ runs locally on your device and never joins the meeting. It's 100% undetectable.

Troubleshooting

BQ isn't working. What should I do?

Try restarting BQ and your meeting application. Make sure you have the latest version installed.
